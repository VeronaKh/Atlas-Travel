Our Story

About Atlas Travel

Who We Are

More Than a
Travel Company

Atlas Travel was born from a simple belief: the best journeys happen when you travel with people who truly know and love the land. We are a small, passionate team of Moroccan guides and international travel specialists who have spent years exploring every corner of this extraordinary country.

Founded in 2012 by Youssef Ait Benhaddou — a Berber mountain guide from the High Atlas — and Lena Fischer, a German travel journalist who fell in love with Morocco on her first visit, we combine deep local knowledge with the practical needs of international travellers.

No crowded buses. No tourist-trap restaurants. No copy-paste itineraries. Just Morocco as it truly is — raw, beautiful, and full of stories waiting to be discovered.

The People

Meet Our Team

The guides, storytellers and planners who make every trip unforgettable.

Youssef Ait Benhaddou
Founder & Head Guide

Born in the Atlas Mountains, Youssef has been guiding treks since he was 19. He speaks Berber, Arabic, French and English — and knows every pass, village and stargazing spot in southern Morocco.

Lena Fischer
Co-Founder & Trip Designer

Former travel journalist from Berlin. Lena first visited Morocco in 2010 and never really left. She designs all itineraries with the eye of a writer and the heart of a traveller.

Amina Benali
Desert Guide & Cultural Host

Amina grew up near Merzouga and leads all Sahara expeditions. She introduces travellers to Berber music, cooking and the profound silence of the desert at night.

Our Commitment

Travel That
Gives Back

We believe travel should benefit the places and people it touches. That's why we work exclusively with locally owned riads, Berber family camps and village cooperatives — ensuring that the money you spend stays in Morocco.

We keep group sizes small to protect fragile desert and mountain ecosystems, and we partner with a reforestation project in the High Atlas that plants 10 trees for every trip we run.
